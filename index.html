Constitution

Adopted: 02 June 2025

1. Name

The name of the association is the Big Bhudha Pablo Cannabis Club, hereafter referred to as "the Club."

2. Legal Nature

The Club is a private, non-profit voluntary association, operating separately with its own legal rights.

3. Purpose
